We are India's fastest growing outsourcing company

Konexions Back Office Services is one of India’s largest leader for business process management services , leveraging extensive domain expertise and future-ready digital platforms to drive client productivity through outsourced solutions.We provide a host of technology-enabled staffing and managed outsourcing services being the leading provider across Banking, Telecom, Insurance, NBFC's, Outsourcing Services, IT, Manufacturing, Retail and more.

Our passion for delivering exceptional services, augmented by proprietary digital platforms like Workflow automation tools; Customer relationship management (CRM) systems; Data analytics and reporting solutions; Mobile workforce management platforms , has strongly established our credentials as India’s largest employer in the private sector and the biggest integrated business services provider in the country.

With 20,000++ employees/associates we serve over 100++ clients. We are proud to achieve this success as a 18-years old organization. We offer a comprehensive suite of technology-driven staffing and managed outsourcing services, Our business strategy is aligned with industry best practices, evolving customer needs, and technological advancements to deliver innovative and efficient solutions.

Established in 2007 with the headquarteres in Delhi, Konexions today has an unmatched geographical presence scaling with more than 50 offices with 50 cities across India, backed by technology intensity and domain specialization to create exceptional service experiences.

Our commitment goes beyond streamlined processes; we empower businesses to thrive in diverse environments. With us, clients can expect innovation, flexibility and tangible results. At Konexions we believe in excellence & we excel in all that the we do.
